FAQs Support Your Cashmere Business

What Are The Benefits Of Choosing Cashmere For Our Luxury Brand?

RESOVON’s custom cashmere is made for high-end brands.

Cashmere is known for its extraordinary softness, providing a luxurious feel that enhances the wearer’s comfort and experience.

Its superior thermal insulation properties make it eight times warmer than regular wool while remaining lightweight, ensuring an elegant drape and three-dimensional effect.

Cashmere fibers’ natural sheen and wrinkle resistance give it a refined, sophisticated appearance.

We insist on sustainable and ethical farming and sourcing high-quality cashmere, which aligns with your responsible luxury brand positioning.

Do You Offer Private Labeling Or Branding Services?

Yes, we offer private labeling and branding services. We can customize the labels, packaging, and branding elements to reflect your brand’s identity, ensuring a seamless customer experience.

As the only cashmere product supplier with an MBA for luxury brands, we can also provide business consulting for your brand based on more than 10 years of experience to help you position it more accurately.
